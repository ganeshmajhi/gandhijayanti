HAPPY GANDHI JAYANTI 2018
Gandhi Jayanti is a national holiday in India celebrated on 2nd October. This day is celebrated in the honor of the birthday of the Father of the nation, Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, popularly known as Mahatma Gandhi or Bapuji. ... He started the 'Satyagraha' (non-violence) movement for the Indian freedom struggle.
 
   
5 FAMOUS QUOTES OF GANDHI
1. You must not lose faith in humanity. Humanity is an ocean; if a few drops of the ocean are dirty, the ocean does not become dirty.
2. Happiness is when what you think, what you say, and what you do are in harmony.
3.Satisfaction lies in the effort, not in the attainment, full effort is full victory.
4. The essence of all religions is one. Only their approaches are different.
5. An ounce of practice is worth more than tons of preaching.
